CROSSFIT

CrossFit is a form of high intensity interval training including cardio, weightlifting and gymnastics movements. It helps form a healthy lifestyle by doing safe and effective exercise.

CrossFit can help you achieve any goal, from weight loss to better performance to just keeping fit! The program works for anyone because it can be adapted for those just starting out or for those chasing their next personal record.

Each day the gym offers a different WOD (workout of the day) that is part of a complete program designed to improve your strength and conditioning. For olympic lifting movements, such as a snatch or clean and jerk, we will coach you to achieve safe and efficient technique which will enable you to get stronger.

KIDS & TEENS

CrossFit Kids & Teens is a method of teaching Greg Glassman's CrossFit to children ages 3-18. Based on the principles of mechanics, consistency and then intensity, our classes emphasise good movement throughout childhood and adolescence. Running, jumping, rolling, pulling, swinging, climbing, crawling, throwing and being upside down are just few of things we will be doing at CrossFit Kids & Teens!

CrossFit Kids is aimed at ages 5 to 11 and CrossFit Teens is aimed at ages 11 to 16.

GYMNASTICS

In CrossFit, body-weight movements are considered gymnastics (e.g., air squat, push-up, pull-up, etc.). We are taking skills from the sport of gymnastics and applying them to workouts. In CrossFit, the gymnastics label is applied any exercise in which you move your body through a range of motion without an external load.

CrossFit uses the floor, still rings, pull-up bars, dip bars, climbing ropes, and other equipment to implement gymnastics training.

Our Gymnasic classes will allow you to hone in on specific movements and build the strength required to successfully complete the skill. Skills include toes-to-bar, handstand walks, nuscle-ups and many more!

MOBILITY

Mobility is commonly described as the range of motion, or movement, around a particular joint or set of joints. Or in other words, how far we can reach, bend or turn. Our mobility is important as it allows us to complete basic day to day moves, but also helps decrese the chance of ingury when taking part in CrossFit.

We run a mobility class once a day where we work on stretching different parts of the body statically and dynamically. Stretching allows us to lengthen, or elongate, the muscles and associated soft tissue.

BEGINNERS

CrossFit is a training program that builds strength and conditioning through extremely varied and challenging workouts. It requires practice to complete the movements safely. CrossFit is designed for everyone and is universally scalabily so we have a beginners class where you can practice the movements and learn how to build up strength sensibly.
